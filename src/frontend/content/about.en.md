# About Book Review

Welcome to Book Review — a platform for book lovers to discover, create, and share thoughtful book reviews.

## Our Mission

We believe every book deserves a thoughtful review. Our platform provides a space where readers can share their perspectives, favorite quotes, and recommendations with a community of fellow book enthusiasts.

## Features

- Create and publish book reviews with rich markdown content
- Highlight your favorite quotes from the books you read
- Upload cover images to make your reviews visually appealing
- Full bilingual support in English and Georgian

## Built With

Book Review is built with modern technologies including Next.js, .NET, and PostgreSQL, deployed on Azure Kubernetes Service.
